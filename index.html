Why am I seeking a Career in Software Development?

The Real Question is Why Not?

One of my main reasons for wanting to be a software developer is to be able to continue learning in a new career in an area I'm passionate about. Additionally, the fact that technology is always changing over time is super interesting to me. Prior to really preparing for this bootcamp I didn't realize how many different coding languages there are and how many different career paths that learning to code could take you in.

Something that was always holding me back was that it was too late. This is not what I went to school for, so I could not make the transition. I am excited to prove that wrong!
